What makes a good F1 Fantasy team?

The best team is not just the seven highest names on the points list. It has to fit your budget, use constructors efficiently, leave enough value for transfers, and put the 2x or 3x boost on the right driver.

Fast way to build one

  • Open the Lineup Optimizer and set your real team budget.
  • Choose a strategy: Max Points, Balanced, Max Value or Budget Builder.
  • Lock any drivers or constructors you already know you want, and exclude picks you do not trust.
  • Run the optimizer, then compare two or three realistic teams in Team Compare before locking in.

What to check before deadline

Before the lock deadline, review updated practice or qualifying information, DNF risk, price-change brackets, and whether a chip changes the best lineup. A team that was best pre-practice may not still be best after fresh weekend data arrives.
